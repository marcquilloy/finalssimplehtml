Welcome

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) plays an important role in modern society. It helps people communicate, access information, and perform tasks efficiently using digital tools.

About ICT

ICT refers to technologies used to handle information and aid communication. This includes computers, mobile phones, the internet, software applications, and networks.

Importance of ICT

ICT improves learning, speeds up communication, and increases productivity. It is widely used in education, business, healthcare, and government services.
